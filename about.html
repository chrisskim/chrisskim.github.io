ABOUT ME

My name is Chris Kim and I'm a graphic designer & front-end web developer

I've graduated from Pacific NW College of Art in 2010 with a BFA in Communication Design. After that, I interned at the Someday Lounge creating Music Posters and Web banners for its music events for a little over a year. Then I've worked at Linktech updating and editing client's wordpress sites for a year and was promoted as a UX, Graphics, and Frontend Developer.

I have worked with Illustrator, Photoshop, and InDesign. I've coded with HTML, CSS, jQuery, and PHP and have recently implemented responsive web design practices to my web projects. I also have some experience with printing and pre-press production .

Some of my hobbies include drawing, playing the viola, hiking, and playing indie games
